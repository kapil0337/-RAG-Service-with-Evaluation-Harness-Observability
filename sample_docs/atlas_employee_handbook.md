# Atlas Dynamics — Employee Handbook (2026)

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. The
company operates a hybrid model: employees are expected in the office three
days a week, on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Monday and Friday may be
worked remotely.

## Leave Policy

- **Annual leave:** 21 days per calendar year, in addition to 12 public holidays.
- **Sick leave:** 10 days per year, no medical certificate required for absences of two days or fewer.
- **Maternity leave:** 26 weeks of paid leave.
- **Paternity leave:** 4 weeks of paid leave.
- Unused annual leave may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next year.

## Probation and Notice

New employees serve a probation period of 6 months. After confirmation, the
notice period for resignation is 60 days. During probation, the notice
period is 15 days.

## Benefits

- **Health insurance:** family floater cover of INR 5,00,000 per year, covering the employee, spouse, and up to two children.
- **Learning budget:** every employee receives INR 40,000 per year for courses, books, certifications, and conferences.
- **Internet allowance:** INR 1,500 per month for remote-work days.

## Code of Conduct

All employees must complete the annual security-awareness training within
30 days of their start date and once every calendar year thereafter.
