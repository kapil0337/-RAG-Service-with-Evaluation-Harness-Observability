ATLAS DYNAMICS — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

About the company
------------------
Atlas Dynamics was founded in 2021 and is headquartered in Bengaluru, India.
The company designs and manufactures autonomous robots for warehouse and
logistics automation. Atlas Dynamics has three offices: Bengaluru
(headquarters), Chennai, and Pune.

Support
-------
Enterprise customers receive 24/7 support. Standard-plan customers receive
support from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST on business days. The support email is
support@atlasdynamics.example and the average first-response time is under
4 hours.

Orders and returns
------------------
Products can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund,
provided they are in original condition. Custom-configured units are
non-returnable. Standard shipping within India takes 5 to 7 business days.

Warranty
--------
All hardware products carry a standard 2-year warranty covering
manufacturing defects. Extended warranty of up to 5 years can be purchased
separately.
