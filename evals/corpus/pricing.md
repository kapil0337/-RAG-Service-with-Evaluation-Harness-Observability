# Aether Cloud Storage - Pricing

Pricing is calculated monthly based on storage class, data volume, and
network egress. All prices are listed in USD per GB per month unless
otherwise noted.

## Storage Pricing

| Storage Class      | Price per GB / month | Minimum Storage Duration |
|---------------------|----------------------|---------------------------|
| Standard            | $0.023                | None                       |
| Infrequent Access   | $0.0125               | 30 days                    |
| Archive             | $0.004                | 180 days                   |

## Retrieval Fees

- Standard: no retrieval fee.
- Infrequent Access: $0.01 per GB retrieved.
- Archive: $0.03 per GB retrieved, plus a retrieval delay of up to 12 hours.

## Free Tier

New accounts receive a free tier that includes 5 GB of Standard storage,
20,000 GET requests, and 2,000 PUT requests per month for the first 12
months after sign-up.

## Network Egress

The first 100 GB of egress per month is free. After that, egress is billed
at $0.09 per GB for transfers to the public internet. Transfers between
Aether services in the same region are always free.

## Billing Support

Billing questions can be directed to billing@aether.example.com. Invoices
are issued on the first day of each month for the previous month's usage.
